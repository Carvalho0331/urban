Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting in touch. No complications.

What types of services does URBAN offer?
URBAN operates in two complementary areas: Industrial Metalwork — including design, fabrication and assembly of metal structures, gates, walkways, industrial equipment and welding — and Waste Management — collection, transport, treatment, recycling and environmental consultancy. We work with the industrial sector, municipal entities and private companies.
Are products made to measure?
Yes. Most of our products — metal structures, containers, trailers, gates and walkways — are fully custom-made to the client's specifications: dimensions, capacity, material, finish and colour. We also have standard ready-to-deliver models for waste containers and mini containers.
How can I request a quote?
The process is simple and free: (1) Fill in our contact form on this website or (2) send a message via WhatsApp to (+258) 824 003 219 with a description of your project. Our technical team will review the request and send a detailed proposal in under 24 business hours — no obligation.
What is the average delivery time?
Lead times vary depending on the project type and complexity. As a guide: Standard containers — ready to deliver; Gates and joinery — 3 to 6 weeks; Metal structures — 4 to 8 weeks; Special projects — as per project brief. All timelines are formally agreed before work begins.
Do you work with companies and individuals?
Yes, we work with both. Our client portfolio includes large industrial companies, oil companies, municipal councils, construction firms, SMEs and private clients. We tailor our proposals and processes to the type and size of each client, always maintaining the same level of quality and technical rigour.
Where are you based and what is your area of operation?
URBAN is headquartered in Pemba, Cabo Delgado, and operates throughout northern Mozambique. Our workshop and field team operate from Pemba, with the ability to travel to other parts of the country as required. For projects outside the city, logistics and travel costs are included in the quote.
Are the materials used certified?
Yes. We exclusively use certified structural steel (A36/A572 or equivalent), approved welding consumables, and industrial-grade epoxy paints and primers. All materials are selected to ensure strength, durability and safety, meeting the technical requirements of our industrial and oil sector clients.
Do you provide maintenance services after delivery?
Yes. We offer preventive and corrective maintenance programmes for metal structures, gates, industrial equipment and containers. Programmes include periodic inspections, anti-corrosion treatment, re-tightening and worn component replacement. Contact us to develop a personalised maintenance plan for your facilities.
